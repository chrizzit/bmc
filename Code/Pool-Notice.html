Dear Guests of Pink Shell,

As we strive to consistently provide the best resort accommodation in Florida, we are excited to announce there will be scheduled updates and enhancements of the White Sands Octopool. This project will begin December 9th and conclude December 16th of 2019. Once completed, the Octopool will have new heating and filtration, a resurfaced interior, and underwater speakers that when swimming under water, you’ll hear music playing.

To accommodate these enhancements, the Octopool and the pool deck area of the White Sands building will be closed during this time. Our goal is to complete this project as seamlessly as possible while enhancing the overall appearance of the Octopool. All other outlets of the resort will be open and will not be affected by this project.

During this time, the Sanibel View pool will be open to guests of all ages. From this area, we will offer an outdoor grill with a special menu, bar service, and towel service. You will also be able to enjoy live music and a variety of activities and events for both children and adults.
Activities, along with food and beverage service, will also be available on the beach.

We appreciate your understanding. If you have any questions, please contact Neil Praznik, Director of Operations, by phone at 239-463-8676 or by email at npraznik@pinkshell.com.

Warmest regards,

The Pink Shell Team
